DHS NoWork!

DHS NoWork! is a notification sharing centre where staff and students can create groups by adding in their classmates, and proceed to post notifications and/or homework reminders to the entire group through a very simple sharing system. To use the app, first either get your friends or teachers to add you into their group, or create a group yourself through the Add New Group Button on the top left of the group page. You can then proceed to add homework and/or notifications, which will then be pushed to all the dunmanians in the group you add.

To facilitate interaction, a comment system will also be implemented where users can post questions to the rest of the group related to the homework / notification at hand, a system where the admin of the group will be able to moderate.

Additional features are still constantly in development, so do please send your feedback and opinions on how good the app works! Thank You!
